DR. ADAM GAZDZINSKI

BOARD CERTIFIED DOCTOR OF PHYSICAL THERAPY

Dr. Adam Gazdzinski

Board Certified Doctor of Physical Therapy

Dr. Adam Gazdzinski received his Doctorate in Physical Therapy from Long Island University, NY. Following completion of the program he worked for two years with inpatient cardiopulmonary pre and post surgery patients gaining expertise in this overlooked area of rehabilitation.

Dr. Gazdzinski is one of very few double-boarded practitioners. After transitioning to private practice, Dr. Gazdzinski embarked on a very successful and patient-centered career path culminating in earning two Board Certifications.

Board Certified Orthopaedic Clinical Specialist (OCS)
Board Certified Clinical Electrophysiology Specialist (ECS)

Excelling in the area of orthopaedic rehabilitation and electrophysiology testing, he had an opportunity to work with a wide variety of diagnoses, including pre and post-operative cases, sports related overuse and traumatic injuries, chronic degenerative conditions as well as acute spine and extremity conditions becoming an expert in these areas. Dr. Gazdzinski regularly attends professional development courses and is fully dedicated to evidence based and patient centered practice. Dr. Gazdzinski is committed to designing an individually tailored rehabilitation protocol for each of his patients optimizing function and recovery.
